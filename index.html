About Me

I'm a software engineer with a passion for React, Redux, and Rails. Currently, I am self-learning React Native, Node.js, and Express. My main project right now is a Spotify inspired music streaming web app, where users can listen to their favorite songs, create playlists, and follow their favorite content. Looking to the future, I plan to hone my skills in React Native and deploy a live app on the app store. Outside of my passion for programming, I am in love with music and art. One of my favorite hobbies is crate digging for under appreciated tracks and adding it to my personal archive for DJ mixes I create.

Dropify

Dropify is a single-page, full-stack web application inspired by Spotify and build with Ruby on Rails and PostgreSQL on the backend, React.js using Redux for the frontend

Music Genre Identifier: What Makes a Genre Unique?

An interactive Javascript D3 scatterplot which maps the musical similarities and differences across 620+ music genres.
